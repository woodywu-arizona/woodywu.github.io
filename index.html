Di Wu (吴迪)

Introduction

I'm a third-year Ph.D. student in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineerning Department in UC San Diego. I'm working in the AstroLab under the instructions of Prof. Aaron Rosengren. My research focuses on the development of state-of-the-art algorithms for dynamics model and simulation to solve space debris problem; discover of dynamics models and trajectories in complicated astrodynamics environment to space trajectory and mission design.

Before my Ph.D. study, I received M.S.E. in Aerospace Engineering from University of Michigan on 2017. I completed my B.S. on 2015 in Space Engineering in Beihang University in China.

Research interests

  • Space Situation Awareness
  • Space Trajectory and Mission Design
  • Machine learning
  • Nonlinear dynamics and control
